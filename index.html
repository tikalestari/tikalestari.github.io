What did you do this past week?

This past week I found myself grabbing a cup of tea and enjoying my last free week of the summer. I recently discovered a website called Pramp.com which is a free online peer-2-peer platform for practicing technical interviews. So how it works is, you're matched with a random person based on availability, practicing needs, and programming language preferences and you use a collaborative code editor to interview that person with a problem given by the website. And vice versa; they interview you back. I did this about 5 times this past week and learned what areas I need to work on as far as prepping for upcoming tech interviews.
